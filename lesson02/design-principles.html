Visual Hierarchy

Williams-Sonoma

williams-sonoma.com

Williams-Sonoma displays a beautiful exmaple of visual hierarchy with its "in your face" video clip of their newest product line. This tactic forces the users eyes to the new product and displays the importance of the marketing tactic for their brand.

White Space and Clean Design

Typeform

typeform.com

Typeform shows a brilliant use of white space on their homepage. The visual hierarchy for the title and subtitle is clear, but only thorugh the use off white space surrounding the type giving visual importance to the headings. This gives the feel of a clean and sophisticated design with elements of modern vibes.

Alignment

Area17

area17.com

Area17's homepage displays a unique layout that ultilizes the design element of alignment. With the left aligned type, left aligned logo and heading all in contrast of their left aligned type yet right aligned positioned navigation bar and bold yellow bottom horiztonal banner, gives a modern feel to their whole brand.
